Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

You can reset your study progress by going to Settings > Data Management > Reset Progress.

Is my data backed up?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We recommend regularly backing up your device to prevent data loss.

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues with the app, please send us an email with details about the problem, including your device model and operating system version.
